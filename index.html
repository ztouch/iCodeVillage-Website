About Us

iCodeVillage is a social enterprise whose exclusive purpose is to provide skills in computer coding and entrepreneurship to students, women and people passionate about ICT, in order to contribute to finding innovative solutions to problems related to the development of Mali. It will make it possible to build and make operational a digital institution which will be the pole of ICT development in Mali focused on skills training. Established in Bamako, this digital institution will serve the whole country and is open to countries in the sub-region.

Our services

Training

Training in new technologies, web development, software development, Android / IOS, Artificial Intelligence, Internet-Of-Things.

Entrepreneurship

Include training in Design Thinking, Mentoring, Body Spirits, pair projects.Training in entrepreneurship, the practice of Pitch, Public Speaking, Leadership Skills.

Learn English

For a good command of computer coding, students must have a thorough knowledge of English. Hence compulsory training in English and also allow students to be bilingual and able to participate in international competitions and conferences.
